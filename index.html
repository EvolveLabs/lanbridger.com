Lanbridger click. click. you're connected.

LAN Bridger is an easy-to-use VPN tool. It lets you be a part of the same local computer network at your job, on the road, or half way around the world. Share files. Administer machines. Access music. Or kick back and play a game. If you can do it locally, LAN Bridger lets you do it remotely. It’s as easy as click, click, you're connected.

LAN Bridger is free and small. It’s simple and doesn’t get in the way. So what on earth are you waiting for? Give it a spin today!

What's it for?

Gaming

LAN Bridger is great for gaming. Broadcast traffic simply works, even on Vista and Windows 7. Best of all, because LAN Bridger uses normal LAN IP addresses like 192.168.x.x and 10.x.x.x, most games work without any extra configuration.

File sharing

Already share files using Windows? LAN Bridger makes those same shares available to anyone on your LAN Bridger network. It doesn't matter if they're down the street or across the country.

Media Streaming

Wish you could access your media collection from anywhere? Run an application like Boxee across your LAN Bridger network and you can. Boxee will work just like it does when you’re home

Just about anything

Just about anything. Since LAN Bridger is completely transparent, you really can use it to do anything you normally do on a LAN. Run an FTP server. Host a SOCKS proxy. Share music using iTunes. It all just works.
